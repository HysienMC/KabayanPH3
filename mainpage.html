Server Rules

For the latest and detailed rules, please visit our Discord server.

Follow these essential rules to keep the community fun and friendly:

No Griefing: Do not destroy or steal other players' builds or items.

Respect Others: Treat all players and staff with kindness and respect.

No Cheating: Hacked clients, exploits, or unauthorized mods are strictly prohibited.

No Spam or Advertising: Avoid excessive messages and do not promote other servers.

Keep Chat Clean: No hate speech, offensive language, or inappropriate topics.

No Unapproved Mods: Only client-side mods that do not provide unfair advantages are allowed.

Respect Builds: Do not modify or destroy other players' structures without permission.

Follow Admin Instructions: Staff decisions are final, and rules may be updated as needed.

Have Fun: Enjoy the game and help create an awesome community!
